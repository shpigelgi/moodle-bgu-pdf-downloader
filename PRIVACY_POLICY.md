# Privacy Policy for Moodle BGU PDF Downloader

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## Overview

Moodle BGU PDF Downloader is a Chrome extension that helps Ben-Gurion University students download course materials from Moodle. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

The extension:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track browsing history
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- Does NOT send data to external servers
- Does NOT store data in the cloud

## Data Processing

The extension processes data locally on your device only:

1. **Course Page Scanning**: Reads Moodle course pages (moodle.bgu.ac.il) to identify downloadable files
2. **File Downloads**: Uses Chrome's download API to save files to your local computer
3. **All processing happens entirely on your device** - no data leaves your computer

## Permissions Justification

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **downloads**: Required to download files from Moodle to your computer
- **activeTab**: Required to read the current Moodle course page content
- **scripting**: Required to scan course pages for file links
- **Host permission (moodle.bgu.ac.il)**: Limited to BGU Moodle only

## Third-Party Services

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted here and reflected in the "Last Updated" date.

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete.
